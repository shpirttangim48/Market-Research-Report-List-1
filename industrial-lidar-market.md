<p><strong>The Industrial LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid State Lidar</li><li>Mechanical/Scanning Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial LiDAR market comprises two main types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes fixed sensors with no moving parts, offering compact, durable, and cost-effective solutions for applications requiring quick data capture. In contrast, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR employs rotating components to capture a wide field of view, providing high-resolution 3D mapping but often at a higher cost and with more maintenance needs. Both types serve diverse industrial applications, including automation, surveying, and safety monitoring.</p></p>
