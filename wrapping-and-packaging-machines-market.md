<p><p>Wrapping and packaging machines are classified primarily into horizontal and vertical markets based on their orientation and application. Horizontal machines operate with products placed horizontally, ideal for packaging items like boxes and bulk goods. They offer efficiency in high-speed production and are commonly used in industries like food and beverage. In contrast, vertical machines work with products positioned vertically, catering to smaller items like pouches and bags, making them suitable for compact spaces and applications such as pharmaceuticals and consumer goods.</p></p>
