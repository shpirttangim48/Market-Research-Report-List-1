<p><p>The patient cooling system market is categorized into three main types: Surface Cooling Systems, Intravascular Cooling Systems, and Cooling Accessories. Surface Cooling Systems utilize external devices to lower body temperature, often through specialized blankets or pads. Intravascular Cooling Systems involve the use of catheters that circulate cooled fluids within the bloodstream for rapid temperature reduction. Cooling Accessories encompass additional tools and equipment that support the main systems, enhancing their effectiveness and patient comfort during therapeutic hypothermia treatments.</p></p>
