<p><p>The botanical native pesticide market encompasses various categories, notably phytotoxin and phytogenous insect hormones. Phytotoxins are naturally occurring toxic compounds derived from plants that target pests, disrupting their physiological processes. In contrast, phytogenous insect hormones mimic natural hormones in insects, influencing their growth, reproduction, and behavior, effectively managing pest populations. Both types offer eco-friendly alternatives to synthetic pesticides, promoting sustainable agriculture and reducing environmental impact while enhancing crop protection efficiency.</p></p>
