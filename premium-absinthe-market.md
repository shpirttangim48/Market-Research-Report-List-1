<p><p>The Premium Absinthe market comprises two primary types: Distillation and Cold Mixing. Distillation is the traditional method, where wormwood and other botanicals are steeped and then distilled, producing a complex and authentic spirit. In contrast, Cold Mixing involves blending pre-extracted botanical essences with alcohol without the distillation process, offering a quicker and often more affordable alternative. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences, with distillation appealing to purists and cold mixing attracting those seeking convenience and innovation in flavor.</p></p>
