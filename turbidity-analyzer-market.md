<p><p>The turbidity analyzer market is segmented into two main types: dry media and wet media. Dry media analyzers utilize powdered or granular substances to assess turbidity levels, offering advantages in portability and ease of use. In contrast, wet media analyzers measure turbidity directly in liquid samples, providing precise and instant readings suitable for continuous monitoring. These two types cater to different application needs, ranging from laboratory testing to real-time industrial process control, enhancing water quality management across various sectors.</p></p>
