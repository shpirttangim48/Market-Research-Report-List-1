<p><p>The Military Aviation Inertial Navigation System market is primarily divided into two types: Single Antenna Inertial Navigation Systems and Dual Antenna Inertial Navigation Systems. Single antenna systems utilize one antenna for navigation, making them simpler and often more cost-effective, suitable for basic applications. In contrast, dual antenna systems employ two antennas to enhance accuracy and provide better positioning capabilities, particularly in complex environments. This differentiation allows military aircraft to choose systems based on operational requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
