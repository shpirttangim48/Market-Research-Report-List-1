<p><p>The tensiometer market is categorized into several types, each serving specific applications in measuring surface tension. Force sensor tensiometers employ mechanical force to gauge tension based on weight measurements. Bubble pressure tensiometers analyze the pressure of bubbles emerging in a liquid. Drop volume tensiometers determine surface tension by measuring the volume of a droplet before detachment. Spinning drop tensiometers assess surface tension through the behavior of a droplet in a rotating environment. Other variants include options tailored for specialized applications.</p></p>
