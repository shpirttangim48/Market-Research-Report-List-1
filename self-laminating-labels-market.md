<p><p>The self-laminating labels market encompasses three main types: polyester, polyimide, and acrylic. Polyester labels are durable, resistant to moisture and chemicals, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Polyimide labels offer exceptional heat resistance and are commonly used in electronics and high-temperature environments. Acrylic labels provide excellent clarity and adhesion, suitable for a variety of surfaces. Each type serves specific industry needs, reflecting different performance characteristics tailored to applications ranging from industrial labeling to consumer products.</p></p>
