<p><p>The Childbirth Monitor market comprises three primary types: Bedside Monitors, Central Monitors, and Discharge Monitors. Bedside Monitors are used in labor and delivery rooms to track vital signs and fetal well-being in real-time. Central Monitors aggregate data from multiple bedside units, allowing healthcare providers to monitor several patients simultaneously from a centralized location. Discharge Monitors focus on post-delivery care, ensuring that new mothers and infants are stable before leaving the hospital, enhancing safety and improving care coordination.</p></p>
