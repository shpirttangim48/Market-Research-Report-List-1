<p><p>The medical ready-to-use syringe market is segmented into two main types: glass syringes and polymer syringes. Glass syringes are durable and exhibit excellent chemical resistance, making them suitable for precise dosing and long-term storage. They are often used for vaccines and injectables. In contrast, polymer syringes, typically made from plastic materials, are lightweight, disposable, and cost-effective, appealing for single-use applications. Both types cater to varying healthcare settings based on factors such as sterility, safety, and patient comfort.</p></p>
