<p><ul><li>Surface Warming Method</li><li>Intravenous Warming Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IV fluid warmer market comprises two primary types: surface warming method and intravenous warming method. The surface warming method uses external heat sources to raise the temperature of IV fluids before administration, promoting patient comfort and preventing hypothermia. In contrast, the intravenous warming method directly heats the fluid as it flows through an insulated device, ensuring optimal warmth at the point of delivery. Both methods are crucial in medical settings to enhance patient safety and care during fluid administration.</p></p>
