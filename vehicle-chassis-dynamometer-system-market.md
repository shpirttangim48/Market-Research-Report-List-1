<p><strong>The Vehicle Chassis Dynamometer System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi Roller</li><li>Single Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Chassis Dynamometer System market comprises two primary types: Multi Roller and Single Roller systems. Multi Roller dynamometers use multiple rollers to simulate road conditions, allowing for more accurate testing of vehicles across various speeds and loads. In contrast, Single Roller systems utilize a single roller for testing, which is simpler and often less expensive but may limit the scope of tests. Both systems serve to assess vehicle performance, emissions, and efficiency, catering to different testing needs and budgets.</p></p>
