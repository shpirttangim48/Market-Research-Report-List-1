UHF amplifiers operate in the 300 MHz to 3 GHz range, providing effective communication in environments like tunnels and large buildings, while VHF amplifiers function between 30 MHz and 300 MHz, commonly used for broadcasting and in public safety applications. Both types are essential for enhancing signal strength and ensuring reliable connectivity in challenging environments.</p></p>
