<p><strong>The Impellers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-Shear</li><li>Medium-Shear</li><li>High-Shear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The impellers market is divided into three main categories based on shear force: low-shear, medium-shear, and high-shear. Low-shear impellers are designed for gentle mixing and are ideal for fragile materials and suspensions. Medium-shear impellers balance agitation while maintaining material integrity, suitable for emulsions and dispersions. High-shear impellers generate significant turbulence, effectively breaking down particles and facilitating homogenization, making them perfect for applications requiring intense mixing, such as in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
