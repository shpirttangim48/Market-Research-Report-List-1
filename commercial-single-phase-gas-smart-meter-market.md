<p><p>The commercial single-phase gas smart meter market is divided into two main types: IC Card and Non-IC Card meters. IC Card meters utilize integrated circuit cards for storing and managing user data, enabling secure and convenient transactions. They allow customers to prepay for gas usage, enhancing energy efficiency. Non-IC Card meters, on the other hand, rely on traditional mechanisms for billing and data collection, often lacking the advanced payment features of IC Card meters. Both serve distinct market needs and user preferences.</p></p>
