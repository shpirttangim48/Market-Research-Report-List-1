<p><ul><li>"Mass Air Flow Sensors"</li><li>"Volume Air Flow Sensors"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC market features two primary types of air flow sensors: Mass Air Flow (MAF) sensors and Volume Air Flow (VAF) sensors. MAF sensors measure the mass of air entering the system, providing precise data crucial for optimal combustion and energy efficiency. In contrast, VAF sensors measure the volumetric flow rate of air, helping to assess and maintain proper ventilation levels. Both types are essential for ensuring effective HVAC operation, enhancing indoor air quality, and optimizing energy consumption.</p></p>
