<p><strong>The PU Foam Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU foam sealant market is categorized into one-component and two-component systems. One-component sealants are pre-formed foams that expand upon exposure to moisture, making them easy to apply without mixing, ideal for simple applications like gaps and cracks. In contrast, two-component sealants require a mixing process of resin and hardener before application, offering superior strength and adaptability for more demanding tasks such as insulation and sealing in construction. Both types serve distinct applications based on performance needs.</p></p>
