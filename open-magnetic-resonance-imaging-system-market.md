<p><strong>The Open Magnetic Resonance Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet</li><li>Superconducting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Open Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) System market comprises two main types: Permanent Magnet and Superconducting MRI systems. Permanent Magnet MRI utilizes fixed magnetic fields created by permanent magnets, offering cost-effective and quieter imaging with limited field strength, suitable for certain diagnostic applications. In contrast, Superconducting MRI systems employ superconducting magnets cooled to extremely low temperatures, generating high magnetic fields that enable detailed imaging of soft tissues and more complex diagnoses, making them ideal for advanced medical use.</p></p>
