<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber For Civil Aviation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN-Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Viscose-Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Pitch-Based Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber market for civil aviation primarily consists of three types: PAN-based, viscose-based, and pitch-based carbon fibers. PAN-based carbon fiber, derived from polyacrylonitrile, is the most prevalent due to its high strength and lightweight properties, ideal for aircraft structures. Viscose-based carbon fiber, produced from regenerated cellulose, offers good tensile strength but is less common in aviation. Pitch-based carbon fiber, made from petroleum pitch, is known for its thermal and electrical conductivity, finding niche applications.</p></p>
