<p><p>The frosted acrylic sheet market is categorized into two main types: one-sided frosted and double-sided frosted sheets. One-sided frosted sheets feature a textured surface on one side, providing a translucent appearance while maintaining clarity on the reverse side, making them ideal for applications needing partial privacy. In contrast, double-sided frosted sheets have a frosted finish on both sides, offering uniform opacity and aesthetic appeal, suitable for decorative uses or partitions that require privacy from all angles.</p></p>
