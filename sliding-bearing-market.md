<p><strong>The Sliding Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Sliding Bearing</li><li>Axial Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sliding bearing market comprises primarily two types: radial sliding bearings and axial sliding bearings. Radial sliding bearings support loads perpendicular to the shaft, allowing rotational movement with minimal friction, making them ideal for applications like electric motors and machinery. In contrast, axial sliding bearings manage loads parallel to the shaft, providing stability in applications such as thrust bearings in turbines and heavy equipment. Both types are crucial for efficient mechanical operation, enhancing performance and extending the lifespan of machinery.</p></p>
