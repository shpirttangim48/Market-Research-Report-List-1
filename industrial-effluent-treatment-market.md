<p><strong>The Industrial Effluent Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-site Treatment</li><li>Off-site Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial effluent treatment market encompasses two primary types: on-site treatment and off-site treatment. On-site treatment involves facilities managing wastewater directly at the source, utilizing technologies such as filtration, chemical treatment, or biological processes to ensure compliance with environmental regulations before discharge. In contrast, off-site treatment involves transporting effluent to specialized treatment facilities, where it undergoes extensive processing. Both approaches aim to mitigate environmental impact, but their choice depends on factors like cost, regulatory requirements, and operational convenience.</p></p>
