<p><p>The market for single-phase water smart meters can be divided into two main types: IC card and non-IC card. IC card smart meters utilize integrated circuit technology, allowing users to recharge and manage their water consumption through a secure, portable card. In contrast, non-IC card meters operate without this technology, typically using mechanical or electronic methods for water billing and measurement. Both types enhance efficiency in water management, but IC card systems offer more advanced features and user control.</p></p>
