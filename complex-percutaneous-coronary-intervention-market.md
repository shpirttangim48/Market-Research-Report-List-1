<p><p>The Complex Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI) market is primarily categorized into Drug-Eluting Stents (DES), Bare Metal Stents (BMS), and Bioresorbable Stents. DES are coated with medication to reduce restenosis rates, making them preferred for complex cases. BMS are simpler, used when prolonged drug exposure is not desired. Bioresorbable Stents are innovative, gradually dissolving over time, ideally restoring natural vessel function without permanent implants. Each type addresses specific clinical needs and preferences, impacting treatment outcomes in coronary artery disease.</p></p>
