<p><p>Multi-part barcode labels encompass various printing methods tailored for different applications. Offset printing provides high-quality images for large runs, while flexography is suited for high-speed production of flexible packaging. Rotogravure offers exceptional print quality for intricate designs, ideal for long runs. Screen printing allows for vibrant colors and unique textures, commonly used on materials requiring durability. Letterpress combines traditional techniques with modern design, offering a tactile feel. Digital printing excels in short runs and customization, ensuring flexibility and fast turnaround.</p></p>
