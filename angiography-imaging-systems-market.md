<p><p>The angiography imaging systems market includes various modalities: </p><p>1. **X-Ray Angiography**: Utilizes X-rays to visualize blood vessels and is widely used for evaluating cardiovascular conditions. </p><p>2. **CT Angiography**: Combines computed tomography technology to provide detailed images of blood vessels, enhancing diagnostic capabilities. </p><p>3. **MRI Angiography**: Employs magnetic resonance imaging to visualize blood flow, making it useful for soft tissue evaluation. </p><p>4. **MR-CT Angiography**: Integrates both MRI and CT techniques for comprehensive vascular assessments, offering high accuracy and detail in imaging.</p></p>
