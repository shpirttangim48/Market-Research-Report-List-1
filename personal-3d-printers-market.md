<p><strong>The Personal 3D Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Poly-jet</li><li>Fuse Deposition Modeling (FDM)</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)</li><li>Stereo Lithography</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The personal 3D printer market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Polyjet** utilizes inkjet technology to deposit layers of photopolymer resin, achieving high precision and vibrant colors. </p><p>2. **Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)** extrudes thermoplastic filament, making it popular for ease of use and affordability. </p><p>3. **Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)** uses a laser to fuse powdered materials, ideal for complex geometries. </p><p>4. **Stereolithography (SLA)** employs UV light to cure resin, resulting in smooth finishes. </p><p>5. **Others** include diverse technologies and custom solutions catering to specific user needs.</p></p>
