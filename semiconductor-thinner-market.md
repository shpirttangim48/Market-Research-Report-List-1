<p><strong>The Semiconductor Thinner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Removal</li><li>Negative Photoresist Removal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor thinner market includes two primary types: positive photoresist removal and negative photoresist removal. Positive photoresist removal involves solvents that dissolve the exposed areas of photoresist, making it suitable for applications requiring high resolution and pattern fidelity. Conversely, negative photoresist removal uses chemicals that dissolve unexposed regions, beneficial for processes needing robust pattern retention. Both methods are essential in microfabrication, ensuring precise pattern transfer in semiconductor manufacturing, and cater to different requirements of the production process.</p></p>
