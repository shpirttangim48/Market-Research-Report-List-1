<p><p>CNC rotary transfer machines are specialized equipment used for automating the machining process in manufacturing. The market is divided into two primary types: vertical axis and horizontal axis machines. Vertical axis machines facilitate operations that require upward or downward motion, improving tool access and reducing the footprint. In contrast, horizontal axis machines optimize space and allow for efficient loading and unloading of parts, making them suitable for larger workpieces. Both types enhance productivity by streamlining manufacturing processes.</p></p>
