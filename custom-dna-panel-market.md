<p><strong>The Custom DNA Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amplicon-based Approach</li><li>Hybridization-based Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Custom DNA Panel market is primarily segmented into two main approaches: amplicon-based and hybridization-based. The amplicon-based approach focuses on selectively amplifying target DNA regions, allowing for precise sequencing and analysis of specific genes or mutations. In contrast, the hybridization-based approach involves capturing and enriching target sequences using labeled probes that bind to complementary DNA segments. Both methods cater to personalized medicine, enabling tailored diagnoses and treatments by offering detailed insights into genetic variations relevant to individual patients.</p></p>
