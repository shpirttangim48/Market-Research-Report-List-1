<p><ul><li>Flame Dried(FD) Fish Meal</li><li>Steam Dried(SD) Fish Meal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Defatted fish meal is categorized into two main types: Flame Dried (FD) Fish Meal and Steam Dried (SD) Fish Meal. FD fish meal is produced by rapidly drying fish with direct flame, resulting in high protein content and often a slightly darker color. In contrast, SD fish meal is created through steam drying, which preserves more fatty acids and maintains a lighter color. Both types are valued in animal feed for their nutritional benefits, particularly in aquaculture and livestock diets.</p></p>
