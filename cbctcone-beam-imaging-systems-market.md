<p><p>CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) systems are categorized into two primary types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical-type systems are designed for compact spaces, allowing for efficient imaging while maximizing patient comfort. They facilitate easier positioning of patients, especially in dental applications. Horizontal-type systems, on the other hand, offer a wider area for imaging, making them suitable for larger anatomical regions and complex cases. Both types serve the growing demand for precise 3D imaging in dentistry, orthopedics, and other medical fields.</p></p>
