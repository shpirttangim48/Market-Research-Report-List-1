<p><p>The high power isolator market is categorized into two primary types: single-stage and dual-stage isolators. Single-stage isolators are designed to provide adequate isolation at lower power levels, offering simplicity and minimal insertion loss. In contrast, dual-stage isolators enhance performance by utilizing two isolation stages, which ensures superior isolation and better handling of higher power levels. This makes dual-stage isolators ideal for demanding applications, providing enhanced reliability and efficiency in environments requiring stringent isolation standards.</p></p>
