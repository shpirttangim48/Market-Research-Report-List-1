<p><p>The whole house vacuum market is primarily divided into two types: bagged and bagless vacuums. Bagged whole house vacuums utilize disposable bags to collect dirt and debris, which helps in trapping allergens and providing easy disposal. In contrast, bagless whole house vacuums rely on canisters or dust cups for collecting debris, offering the advantage of frequent monitoring and easy emptying. Both types cater to different consumer preferences regarding maintenance, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
