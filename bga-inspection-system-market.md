<p><p>The BGA Inspection System market comprises three main types: Electrical Testing, Optical or Visual Inspection, and X-Ray Inspection. Electrical Testing evaluates the integrity and functionality of Ball Grid Array connections, ensuring proper electrical performance. Optical or Visual Inspection uses cameras and imaging techniques to detect surface defects and alignment issues. X-Ray Inspection provides a detailed view of internal structures, identifying solder joint quality and hidden flaws. Together, these methods enhance the reliability and performance of electronic assemblies.</p></p>
