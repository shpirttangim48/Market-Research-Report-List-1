<p><p>The unilateral deafness treatment devices market is categorized into three main types: Air Conducting Hearing Aids, Bone Conducting Hearing Aids, and Others. Air Conducting Hearing Aids amplify sound and transmit it through the ear canal, making them suitable for those with conductive hearing loss. Bone Conducting Hearing Aids vibrate the skull to bypass damaged areas, effectively targeting those with sensory-neural hearing impairments. The "Others" category includes various emerging technologies and alternative devices designed to assist individuals with unilateral hearing loss.</p></p>
