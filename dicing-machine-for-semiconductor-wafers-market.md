<p><p>The dicing machine market for semiconductor wafers primarily includes two types: dicing saws and laser saws. Dicing saws utilize a diamond-tipped blade to cut wafers into smaller pieces with high precision, ideal for traditional semiconductor applications. In contrast, laser saws employ focused laser beams to slice wafers, offering advantages in speed and minimal mechanical stress, making them suitable for delicate materials. Both technologies are crucial in the semiconductor manufacturing process, ensuring efficient and accurate wafer separation.</p></p>
