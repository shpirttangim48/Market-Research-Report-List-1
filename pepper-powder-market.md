<p><strong>The Pepper Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Pepper Powder</li><li>Black Pepper Powder</li><li>Green Prickley Ash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pepper powder market is primarily divided into three types: white pepper powder, black pepper powder, and green prickly ash. White pepper powder, made from mature pepper berries with the outer skin removed, offers a milder flavor and is often used in light-colored dishes. Black pepper powder, derived from dried unripe berries, is known for its robust flavor and is widely used in various cuisines. Green prickly ash, less common, has a unique citrusy flavor and adds a distinct aroma to culinary applications.</p></p>
