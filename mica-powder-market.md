<p><ul><li>Wet Ground</li><li>Dry Ground</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mica powder market is categorized into two main types: wet ground and dry ground mica. Wet ground mica is produced using water, yielding finer particles with enhanced brightness and lower impurities, making it ideal for cosmetics and paints. Dry ground mica, on the other hand, is processed without water, resulting in coarser particles that are often used in applications like plastics and construction materials due to their durability. Both types serve distinct industrial needs, influencing their market demand and pricing.</p></p>
