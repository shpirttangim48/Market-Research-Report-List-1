<p><p>A jet mill is a specialized milling equipment that uses high-velocity air or steam to achieve particle size reduction without the use of heavy machinery. It operates on the principle of fluid energy, where particles are accelerated in a jet of air, colliding with each other to break down into finer particles. This technology is widely employed in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemicals, due to its capacity for producing ultra-fine powders with narrow particle size distributions and minimal contamination.</p><p>The Jet Mill Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for finer and more consistent powders across multiple sectors.
