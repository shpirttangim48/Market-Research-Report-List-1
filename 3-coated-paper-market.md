<p><p>The coated paper market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical and woodfree. Mechanical coated papers are produced using a combination of mechanical pulp and coatings, offering cost-effectiveness and good printability, making them suitable for various applications, such as magazines and brochures. Woodfree coated papers, however, are made from chemical pulp, resulting in a smoother finish and higher brightness, ideal for premium printing needs like catalogs and packaging. Both types cater to different market demands based on quality and application.</p></p>
