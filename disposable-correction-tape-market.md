<p><p>The Disposable Correction Tape market is primarily divided into two types: paper-based and polyester film-based. Paper-based correction tape utilizes a paper substrate for its application, offering a smoother writing experience and better adhesion to various types of paper. In contrast, polyester film-based correction tape features a durable, plastic-like material that provides enhanced strength and resistance to tearing. Both types cater to different user preferences, with paper-based options favored for traditional use and polyester film-based types preferred for their durability and versatility.</p></p>
