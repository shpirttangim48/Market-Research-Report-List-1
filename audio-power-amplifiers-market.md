<p><ul><li>Class-A</li><li>Class-B</li><li>Class-A/B</li><li>Class-G&H</li><li>Class-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio power amplifier market includes several types based on their design and efficiency. Class-A amplifiers offer high sound quality but are less efficient. Class-B amplifiers are more efficient but may introduce distortion at lower levels. Class-A/B combines both, providing a balance of quality and efficiency. Class-G and Class-H amplifiers enhance efficiency by using multiple power supplies, adapting to signal levels. Class-D amplifiers utilize switching technology for high efficiency, making them suitable for modern applications with space and power constraints.</p></p>
