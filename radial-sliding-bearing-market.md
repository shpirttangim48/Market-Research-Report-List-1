Furthermore, the shift towards environmentally sustainable solutions is propelling the development of grease-free and low-friction bearing options. <p><ul><li>Integral Sliding Bearing</li><li>Split Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radial sliding bearing market is primarily categorized into two types: integral sliding bearings and split sliding bearings. Integral sliding bearings consist of a single continuous structure, providing robust support and maintaining alignment within machinery. In contrast, split sliding bearings feature a divided design, allowing for easier installation and maintenance. Both types are used in various industrial applications, offering efficient load distribution and reduced friction, which enhances performance and prolongs the lifespan of rotating equipment.</p></p>
