<p><p>Radio transmission probes for machine tools are essential in enhancing precision and efficiency in manufacturing processes. The market is segmented into two main types: workpiece probes and tool probes. Workpiece probes are designed to measure the position and dimensions of the part being machined, ensuring accurate machining. Tool probes, on the other hand, are used to measure the tool's geometry and wear, allowing for optimal tool management and maintenance. Both types improve manufacturing accuracy and reduce downtime.</p></p>
